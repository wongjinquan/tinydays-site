Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the widget to my home screen?

Long-press on your home screen, tap the + button in the top-left corner, search for "Tiny Days", choose your preferred widget size, and tap "Add Widget". You can then drag it to your preferred position.

How do I add another child?

Open Tiny Days, tap the gear icon in the top-right corner to open Settings, then tap "Add child". You can switch between profiles by tapping the name at the top of the home screen.

How do I change the widget theme?

Open the app, tap the gear icon, and select "Theme". Choose from palettes like Soft Cloud, Blossom, Midnight, Sage, Sunshine, Lavender, and Ocean. The widget updates automatically.

Does Tiny Days collect my data?

No. All data stays on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no tracking. Read our full privacy policy for details.

Why isn't my widget updating?

iOS controls when widgets refresh. If your widget seems stuck, try removing and re-adding it. Make sure Tiny Days hasn't been offloaded by going to Settings > General > iPhone Storage.
